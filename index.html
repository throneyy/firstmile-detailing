FAQ
Questions? We've Got Answers
Do I need to be home during the detail?
Nope! As long as we have access to your vehicle and a water source, you're free to go about your day. Many of our clients book while they're at work and come back to a showroom-clean ride.
How long does a detail take?
The Essential Clean takes about 1.5-2 hours, the Deluxe Detail runs 2.5-3 hours, and the Ultimate Protection is typically 3.5-4.5 hours depending on vehicle size and condition.
What areas do you serve?
We serve Staunton, Waynesboro, Harrisonburg, Lexington, Fishersville, Verona, Bridgewater, and most of Augusta, Rockingham, and Rockbridge counties. If you're not sure, just ask — we'll make it work.
What products do you use?
We use only professional-grade, pH-balanced products that are safe for all paint types, leather, vinyl, and trim. No harsh chemicals, no shortcuts. We treat your car like it's our own.
Do you charge more for SUVs and trucks?
Pricing starts at the listed rate for sedans/coupes. Midsize SUVs are +$25 and full-size trucks/SUVs are +$50 due to the additional time and product required. We'll confirm exact pricing when you book.
Do you need access to water and electricity?
A standard outdoor water spigot is all we need. We bring our own power, equipment, and supplies. If water access is tricky, let us know and we'll figure out a solution.
